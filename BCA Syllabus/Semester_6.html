SEMESTER-VI

BCA-601:- Project Report:- A project work will be done by all the students separately or in a group containing 4 or 5 students on a topic assigned by the Guide(any of the faculty of the College). The student will complete their project under the supervision of the Guide and submit the report in hard and soft copy at the time of evaluation.
BCA-602:- Seminar:-A Departmental Seminar will be organised and students will have to present a paper in the seminar.
BCA-603:- Viva:-A viva-voce of 50 Marks will be conducted by the external examiners(appointed by the University/Examination Board) covering the papers studied by students in 1st to 5th Semesters.
